About Us

We are TransportApp, a smart logistics solution for companies and transporters. Our platform allows you to monitor real-time data such as the temperature and weight of the cargo using IoT devices. Additionally, you can view detailed graphical reports over time, ensuring better management and decision-making.

Our mission is to revolutionize the logistics industry by offering seamless and real-time monitoring, providing businesses and transporters with insights and tools to improve their operations.

Benefits

Real-time monitoring

Thanks to the IoT devices integrated into TransportApp, you can monitor the temperature and weight of the transport in real time, ensuring the quality of your products during transport.

Information graphics

Visualize the performance of your shipments over time with detailed graphs based on collected data, allowing you to make informed decisions and improve your logistics.

Automatic notifications

Get real-time alerts on any changes in transport conditions or shipment status, so you can take immediate action when necessary.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is TransportApp?

TransportApp is a platform that allows companies to monitor their shipments in real time using IoT devices, providing key data such as temperature and weight.

How do IoT sensors work?

Los sensores IoT instalados en los vehículos envían datos en tiempo real sobre la temperatura y el peso del transporte, permitiendo a las empresas mantener el control de su carga durante el envío.

Can I cancel my plan at any time?

Yes, you can cancel or change your plan at any time. There is no penalty for canceling.
